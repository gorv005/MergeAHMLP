    Timeline / Time Map of Luman Group

    1978


    Luman Group laid its foundation in the year 1978 from Delhi.

    1982


    In this year Luman Auto Light Pvt Ltd was established and 2 plants were established in Delhi at Udyog Nagar and Peeragarhi for the manufacturing of Automotive Lights.

    1983


    Luman started Manufacturing of Oil Bath Air Cleaners for OEMs and the Aftermarket in the year 1983. This was then followed by auto electrical products like Combination Switches and other Switch segment products.
