• ABOUT US:

    Founded in 1978, Luman Group is a TS - 16469:2009 and ISO - 9001:2008 certified leading manufacture of automotive components for HCV, LCV and off-road applications in India. With its corporate office in New Delhi, the group achieved a turnover of around USD 14 million in the previous financial year. We currently manufacture various products from four different locations in Northern India. Luman Automotive Systems Private Limited (formerly Luman Auto Light Private Limited) was founded in 1978 by Avtar Singh Sethi, the Firms Principal investor, with over 34 years of experience in the automotive Industry. Mr. Sethi s passion for the automotive industry includes lighting and electrical components as well as engine induction air filtration systems.
